Yelp! Business Over Time

Just a few years ago, we would never have thought that we would be asking total stranger on the internet for reconditions
for restaurants, stores, pet services, etc. But, with the our growing dependency on the apps in our smartphones, that is
exactly what happening. “Yelp it” has become a phrase that we all use in our daily lives when faced with the hassle of
making a decision for what to do or where to go. Yelp has became our saving grace and trusted allay even when we travel to
a country who's language we don't speak.

This project was done during a d3 class in the Information Design and Visualization MFA program in Northeastern University
under the mentorship of Siqi Zhu by the students Maaria Assami and Xiaxin Chen. The visualizations is based on data given by
Yelp and aims to explore the changing star-rates given to the different businesses on the app over three years timeframe.
